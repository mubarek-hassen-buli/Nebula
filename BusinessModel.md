# Nebula Delivery - Business Model

## üìã Executive Summary

Nebula Delivery is a mobile food delivery platform connecting customers with local restaurants. The app provides a seamless ordering experience with features like scheduled orders, reward points, and multi-language support.

---

## üéØ Value Proposition

### For Customers
- **Convenience**: Order food from multiple restaurants in one app
- **Flexibility**: Order now or schedule for later
- **Rewards**: Earn points on every order, redeem for free meals
- **Accessibility**: Multi-language support (English, Amharic, Oromo)
- **Transparency**: Real-time order tracking and status updates

### For Restaurant Partners
- **Increased Reach**: Access to a larger customer base
- **Digital Presence**: Online menu and ordering system
- **Order Management**: Centralized dashboard for managing orders
- **Analytics**: Insights on order patterns and customer preferences

## üí∞ Revenue Streams

### 1. Commission-Based Model (Primary)
| Order Value | Commission Rate |
|-------------|-----------------|
| $0 - $20 | 15% |
| $20 - $50 | 12% |
| $50+ | 10% |

### 2. Restaurant Subscription (Optional)
| Plan | Monthly Fee | Benefits |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Basic | Free | Standard listing |
| Premium | $49/month | Featured placement, analytics |
| Enterprise | $99/month | Priority support, promotions |

### 3. Delivery Fees
- Customers pay delivery fee per order
- Fee varies by distance and order size
- Platform takes percentage of delivery fee

### 4. Promotional Services
- Featured restaurant listings
- In-app banner advertisements
- Push notification campaigns for restaurants

### 5. Future Revenue (Phase 2)
- Subscription meal plans for customers
- Corporate accounts for businesses
- White-label solutions for restaurant chains

- 500+ restaurant partners
- 50,000+ active customers
- Explore additional revenue streams

## üéØ Competitive Advantages

2. **Multi-Language**: Amharic, English, Oromo support

## üìù Summary

Nebula Delivery operates on a commission-based marketplace model connecting customers with local restaurants. The platform generates revenue primarily through order commissions while providing value to both customers (convenience, rewards) and restaurants (digital presence, increased orders). Growth strategy focuses on establishing strong local presence before expanding regionally.
